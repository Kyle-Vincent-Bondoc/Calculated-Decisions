Operation Guide

  • + Addition
  • - Subtraction
  • * Multiplication
  • / Division
  • ** Exponentiation (requires another operation)
  • % Modulus (requires another equation)
  • ! Factorial (e.g., 5! = 120)
  • sum Summation (e.g., sum(5) = 1+2+3+4+5 = 15)
